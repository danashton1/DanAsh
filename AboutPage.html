About this game

Memory tester was developed to challenge peoples ability to memorise the colour sequence of the flashing hue lights in the Smeaton 109 lab.
The player starts off with three lives and will lose a life every time they guess the sequence incorrectly. The game will get progressively harder
by increasing the length of the sequence for the player to remember.
